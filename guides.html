Game Guides

Learn tips, tactics, and progression strategies for the Agee Games arcade collection. These guides are built for players who want to improve their scores, find secrets, and enjoy each browser game more deeply.

Maze Runner Strategy

Maze Runner is all about speed and memory. Start by scanning the path ahead and memorizing the next junction before moving. Use the walls to block line-of-sight from hazards, and collect bonus pickups on your way through the maze.

  • Keep movement smooth: small adjustments are faster than repeated stops.
  • Watch the mini-map and use corners as reset points for timing.
  • Prioritize the fastest route over every collectible on your first run.

Depths of Ashenveil Tips

Depths of Ashenveil combines combat and exploration. Always be ready to dodge, then counterattack. Pick up new weapons and armor as soon as you can, and learn enemy patterns so you can defeat them with fewer hits.

  • Use ranged options to thin out crowds before they reach you.
  • Defeated opponents often drop better equipment—clear rooms carefully.
  • Rest between fights to study the next room layout and prepare your route.

Spear Fisher Gameplay

Spear Fisher is timing-based action on the water. Aim carefully, then strike only when fish are closest. Build up combos by chaining successful catches and avoid missing the next target.

  • Track incoming fish movement before committing to a throw.
  • Focus on precision over power—the fastest catches score best.
  • Use calmer moments between waves to reposition and prepare for the next challenge.
